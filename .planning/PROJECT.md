# Rohan's Professional Portfolio

## What This Is

A professional portfolio website for a Senior Frontend Developer to showcase skills, experience, and projects to recruiters and hiring managers. The portfolio presents Rohan's progression from Junior to Senior Frontend Developer at Logicwind, with a focus on React, TypeScript, and modern frontend technologies.

## Core Value

Make recruiters want to hire Rohan almost instantly by presenting a stunning, professional showcase of his frontend engineering skills through beautiful design, smooth interactions, and comprehensive presentation of his technical expertise and professional journey.
